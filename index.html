About Me

I am Julius Kivengyere, a dedicated community leader with a vision for a better Kicuzi Subcounty. My commitment to serving the people is driven by a passion for community development, social justice, and human rights. I believe in empowering our community through education, infrastructure development, and sustainable practices.

Experience

Community Organizer

Throughout my journey as a community organizer, I have dedicated myself to understanding the needs and aspirations of our residents. I have actively engaged with diverse groups, listening to their concerns and working collaboratively to develop solutions that enhance our community's well-being.

Volunteer Coordinator

As a volunteer coordinator, I have mobilized individuals to contribute their time and skills for the greater good. By empowering volunteers, I have witnessed firsthand the transformative impact of collective action.

Youth Mentor

Through mentorship programs, I have provided support and resources to help young people navigate their educational and personal journeys, empowering them to become active, engaged citizens.

My Vision

Community Development

I am committed to enhancing the quality of life in Kicuzi Subcounty through sustainable development initiatives that address the needs of our residents.

Education and Empowerment

Education is the cornerstone of progress. I will work to improve educational facilities and provide resources to empower our youth and future generations.
